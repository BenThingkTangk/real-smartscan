The SmartScan deal score (0–100) is calculated by our AI engine using multiple weighted factors:

  • Price vs. 90-day average (40% weight): How far below the historical average is the current price?
  • Recency (25% weight): How recently was this price verified? A price from 2 minutes ago scores higher than one from yesterday.
  • Availability confidence (20% weight): Is the item actually in stock?
  • Seller credibility (15% weight): Retailer reputation, return policy quality, and verified seller status

Score guide: 80–100 = Exceptional deal. 60–79 = Good deal. 40–59 = Fair price. Under 40 = At or above historical average.

Scores are recalculated every 5 minutes as new price data arrives. You can always see the score explanation by clicking the score badge on any product.
