Revenue Model — How SmartScan Earns (Without Charging Users)
Transaction Margin
5–15% embedded take-rate on every purchase routed through SmartScan. User sees only savings.

Brand placements in scan results — CPM + CPC. Higher CPM for category-locked inventory.
BNPL Referral
1–3% referral fee per funded BNPL or micro-loan at checkout. Grows with transaction volume.
Resale Fee
4–10% platform fee on every item sold through the resale channel. Scaled by sale price tier.
