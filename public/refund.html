7-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

If you're not satisfied with Prime Elite, request a full refund within 7 days of your initial charge and we'll issue a complete refund — no questions asked.

Standard Cancellation Terms

  • Cancel Anytime: You may cancel your Prime Elite subscription at any time through your Account Dashboard. There are no cancellation fees.
  • Access Continues: Upon cancellation, your Prime Elite access continues until the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged for the next billing cycle.
  • No Pro-Rated Refunds: After the 7-day satisfaction window, we do not issue pro-rated refunds for partial billing periods. Your access simply continues until your billing period ends.
  • No Renewal Charges: Cancelled subscriptions are never renewed. We send a renewal reminder at least 3 days before any charge.

How to Cancel

  1. Log into your account and go to Dashboard → Subscription
  2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
  3. Confirm cancellation — it takes effect immediately
  4. You'll receive a confirmation email within minutes

Alternatively, email support@real-smartscan.com with the subject line "Cancel Prime Elite" from your registered email address.

SmartScan Direct (Buy Here) — Physical Goods
30-Day Return Window

Return Eligibility

Physical goods purchased through SmartScan Direct are eligible for a full refund if returned within 30 days of the delivery date, provided the following conditions are met:

  • Item is in its original, unused condition
  • All original packaging, tags, and accessories are included
  • Item has not been damaged, altered, or used
  • Return request is initiated within the 30-day window

Non-Returnable Items

  • Digital goods, software, or digital download codes
  • Items marked "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" at time of purchase
  • Perishable goods
  • Items damaged due to customer misuse or modification
  • Items returned after the 30-day window

Return Shipping

The buyer is responsible for return shipping costs unless the item was received damaged, defective, or was shipped in error by us. We recommend using a trackable shipping method. We are not responsible for returns lost in transit without a tracking number.

Refund Process

1. Initiate Return (within 30 days of delivery)

Email support@real-smartscan.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll respond with a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and return shipping address within 2 business days.

2. Ship the Item

Package the item securely with all original contents and write the RMA number on the outside of the package. Ship to the address provided.

3. Inspection

Once we receive the item, we'll inspect it within 3–5 business days.

4. Refund Issued

Upon approval, a full refund (less return shipping) will be issued to your original payment method within 5–10 business days. You'll receive an email confirmation when the refund is processed.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you received a damaged or defective item, contact us within 7 days of delivery at support@real-smartscan.com with photos of the damage. We will cover return shipping and issue a full refund or replacement at no additional cost to you.

How to Request a Refund

For all refund requests:

Email: support@real-smartscan.com
Subject: "Refund Request — [Order # or Account Email]"
Include: Your registered email, order number or transaction ID, and reason for request

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 2 business days. For subscription refund requests within the 7-day window, refunds are typically processed within 5–7 business days after approval.

Chargebacks

We encourage you to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card issuer. We process legitimate refund requests quickly, and chargebacks can delay resolution. Accounts with disputed chargebacks may be suspended pending investigation.
